Charlie Flay

Charlie Flay grew up in London and moved to Bristol aged 18 to study Theology. After graduating in 2005 she later trained at the University of Bristol's Acting Academy. Since then she has been in various theatre productions and has been a part of the Fat Shirley's Family since 2007.
