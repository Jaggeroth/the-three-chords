Bob Pepper

As a Chartered Architect, Bob spent his career working in the public sector, including twenty years in the NHS developing new hospitals. He now devotes his time working pro bono for the Bristol Folk House Charity and for the Prince's Trust as a young person mentor. Bob took up the mandolin when he moved to Bristol ten years ago and joined the Bristol Mandolin and Guitar Ensemble where he first learned to read music. Playing with Hogranch, the bluegrass and americana band, has taken him to festivals around the UK, the Netherlands and the USA as well as the now well established relationship with the Three Chords Theatre Company and Fat Shirley.
