Keith Keith

Keith is one quarter of The Hog Ranch taking guitar and vocal duties in the band. Working through the 1990's in a country rock band called The Hanks, a chance meeting led him to discover the world of Bluegrass. Put chance together with meeting Sean Hog Hogan and Bob (MandoBob) Pepper and you get sublime harmonies and cracking acoustic melodies. As well as writing songs for Hog Ranch and working on adaptations for the theatre, Keith is the musical director for Fat Shirley and the alter ego of "Futon" Breezeway.
