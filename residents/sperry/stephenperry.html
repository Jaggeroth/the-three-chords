Stephen Perry

Stephen is a late developer and trained at the Bristol University Acting Academy as recently as 2007/08. His credits include The Mayor in "Richard III" at the Alma Tavern in late 2009, the Ferryman in "Bristol Docks", an interactive film, 2009, Howard Bloxby Hurlington in "Gert Lush", a radio play for BBC Radio Bristol, 2009 and Duncan Moore in the Paper Cage, a Bristol University film, 2009. He hails from Merseyside but spent several years living and working in Atlanta Georgia.
