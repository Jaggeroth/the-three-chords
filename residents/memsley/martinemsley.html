Martyn Emsley

A graduate from Bath Spa University with a degree in performing arts/acting for the recorded media. His hunger for acting began when he left school to join SWADA (south academy of dramatic arts) at Filton college, where he performed in a variety of shows. He first saw Fat shirley at the Alma Tavern when he was 17, and later became part of the 3 chords theatre company a couple of years later.
