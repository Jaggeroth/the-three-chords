Ollie Evans

Ollie is a student at Writhlington sixth form studying BTEC Drama level 3 and BTEC Media studies level 3 in his final year. He wishes to pursue a career in acting by firstly studying at a drama school and then he wishes to go onto Broadway and then into films. Ollie has been performing for the majority of his life, and finding new parts and new characters to get into excites him as he finds it a challenge.
