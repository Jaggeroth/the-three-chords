Sean Hogan

Sean is an accomplished musician whose instrument of choice is the banjo. He has been plucking tunes for longer that he cares to admit and is the front man for The Hog Ranch, a Bristol base blue grass four piece band. Fat Shirely is Sean's first introduction to playing and acting at the same time.
