Tom Turner

Tom studied Peforming arts at Bristols Filton College, and went on to complete an HND in Theatre. He has worked with many companies locally and throughout the country. Over the last few years, as well as appearing in Fat Shirley's, he has appeared in shows such as Breathing Corpses (Plain Clothes Theatre) Twelfth Night (Roughouse Theatre) Can't Pay Won't Pay (Roughouse Theatre) Eddie Kings Tour of The Lost Souls (Edible Theatre) and Christmas Ham (The New Writing Company). Tom also teaches acting at Filton College's South West Academy of Dramatic Arts, and gigs regularly in Bristol and the South West with The Ten Pound Suit Band.
