Tina Jannello

Tina is the only American in the Fat Shirley Family although a northerner! She was born in New York and trained in NYC at the Centre for Media Arts in speech and broadcasting. It was while in Bristol she explored her love for theatre performance. After acting and working behind the scenes with local theatre companies she joined forces with a talented group of musicians and actors to create The Three Chords Theatre Company. Fat Shirley is very close to her heart, having been a part of "the trailer park family" from the beginning and hopes you enjoy it as much as she does.
